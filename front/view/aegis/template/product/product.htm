The Pictures on the Web are misleading. I expected them to follow straight down from my armpits, but they don't. Unfortunately, it gave me an accentuated muffin-top from the front look that was a deal breaker. I'm returning this item.
The fabric was very soft, but I questioned it's durability. It looked like it would have been warm. Also, I ordered the "brilliant Blue Heather" and the color was good, but a much more muted version that I expected from the photos online.
If TNF can shift the ruching to the true side of the garment next time, they would have a great sweatshirt.
